About Us

Shree Sairam PG was established in 2013 and it is part of Shree Sairam Hospitality LLP. We started PG in Airoli for those who are facing many problems like paying brokerage, paying many bills and struggling to make food. It is the first South Indian PG in Airoli and started with 4 girls.

We aim to provide best paying guest and Telugu hostels along with South-Indian food facilities in Airoli.

Shree Sairam Pg and hostels are currently based in Airoli i.e IT hub of Navi Mumbai. Airoli is located 25km from Mumbai airport and easily reachable by Mumbai suburban network. We are providing paying guest and hostel facilities very near to companies such as Capgemini, Accenture, IBM, Wipro, L&T, Axis Bank, UBS, Pharma companies and many more in MindSpace Airoli.
